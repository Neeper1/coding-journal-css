My learning journey as a coding student

My path to being a coding student was anything but linear. Thus began a confusing and challenging year, but now I'm making progress and am able to produce projects that I'm proud of.

How I stay committed to learning

I was always told that to get over writers block, you need to pick a place where you feel comfortable and are not easily distracted. Once you find that place, go everyday and make yourself sit there, regardless of how productive or not you were that day. The main goal at first is habit formation not productivity because one stems from the other.

I adopted this same mindset with coding path. Once I formed a habit, it was easier to get in a productive mindset to commit learning how to code.

How I got started

At first, I was just using pen and paper to record my progress during my coding journey. Once I started my fifth notebook in 3 months, I realized I needed a more sustainable way to record my progress and my notes from my studies--which resulted in this online journal.

This journal helped me grow what I was learning because it kept me motivated to continue posting my summaries which kept me accountable and on-track.
