Hi there! I'm Hannah and welcome to my learning journal.

After several months of learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I am 42% of the way done with my front-end development Scrimba course and I am now able to create responsive websites!

A brief background

A career in coding was not on my radar, to say the least. I got my bachelors degree in political science and my masters degree in international development, so definitely not the start of a typical coding career. However, I soon found out after getting a policy job in Washington, DC that this was not the life I wanted and after two years I decided it was time for a change.

I didn't realize just how big of a change I was going to make, but through a trial and error process, and after months of trying different coding courses, I finally found Scrimba's front-end comprehensive course and now I'm well on my way towards working as a front-end developer.
